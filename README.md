### 🧪 Get `SESSION_NAME` from below:

[![GenerateString](https://img.shields.io/badge/repl.it-generateString-yellowgreen)](https://replit.com/@HEXOROP/eSportMusic) ``Pyrogram``

#### Admins Only 👷‍♂️
- `/pause` - pause song play
- `/resume` - resume song play
- `/skip` - play next song
- `/end` - stop music play
